Biography

Zhang Xiaoming, male, Han nationality, is a native of Taixing City, Jiangsu Province. He was born in 1963, graduated from Southwest University of Political Science and Law with a Bachelor Degree in 1984, from People’s University of China with a Master Degree in Law in 1986. From 2004 to 2012 he served as Deputy Director of Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office of the State Council.

He was an Alternate Member of the 18th CPC Central Committee and Director of the Central Government's Liaison Office in Hong Kong from 2012 to 2017. In 2017 he became Director of the Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office of the State Council and a member of the 19th CPC Central Committee.
