Sean "agro" R. Eberhart is a Republican member of the Indiana House of Representatives, representing the 57th District since 2007. He served on the Shelby County Council from 1998 to 2006 and as its president from 2003 to 2006.[1] He served on the County Council's 4th district.[2] He retired from political life in 2022.

In 2023 he was convicted of corruption.[3]

Early life and education[edit]

Eberhart attended Shelbyville High School.
