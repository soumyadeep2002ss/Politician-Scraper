Summary
Shantanu Mukherjee, Director of the Economic Analysis and Policy Division and Hamid Rashid, Chief of the Global Eonomic Division Monitoring Branch, Economic Analysis and Policy Division (UN DESA) on the launch of the World Economic Situation and Prospects Report 2024
Description

Mr. Mukherjee, and Mr. Rashid will brief on the launch of the World Economic Situation and Prospects Report 2024. This UN flagship economic report presents an outlook for the global economy and underscores the importance of global cooperation and prudent policies to lift global growth and accelerate progress towards the SDGs.
