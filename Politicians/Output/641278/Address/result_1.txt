Information or: Nicolás Trotta
My name is Nicolás Trotta.
I currently live in Vicente López, I am a lawyer, doctorate in education and university teacher.
Former Minister of Education of the Nation.
Previously I worked as a rector of the Metropolitan University for Education and Work (UMET).
I promoted the creation of the Workers' Innovation Center (Citra) with CONICET.
I occupied various positions in the APN (Undersecretary of Management Technologies of the JGM, director of the National Government School, among others).
