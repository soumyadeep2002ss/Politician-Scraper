Carlo Migliaccio
 
Group of belonging: Popolari-Udeur
Carlo Migliaccio was born in Naples in 1960. engaged
From a very young age in volunteering, he works in
Administrative Department of the Cardarelli hospital.
Began political activity in 1983 as
Circumscriptional councilor of Chiaiano, a position he has
still covered in 1987 and which saw it
engaged in different battles to improve the
livability of the neighborhood. As founder and
President of the Cultural Association “City Center
Hilly ”has dealt with the risk problem
hydrogeological of the Camaldoli hill and the relaunch
of the Park of the hilly area.
Elected municipal councilor in 1993 and re -elected in
2006, is president of the "Environment e
Territory".
