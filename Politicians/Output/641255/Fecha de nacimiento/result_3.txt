Blanca Jiménez Castillo
Born	26 September 1965 (age 58)
Puebla, Puebla, Mexico
Occupation	Deputy
Political party	 PAN

Blanca Jiménez Castillo (born 26 September 1965) is a Mexican politician affiliated with the PAN. She currently serves as Deputy of the LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress representing Puebla.[1]
