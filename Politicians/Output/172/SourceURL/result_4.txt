Patna: With the strength of the Bihar Cabinet Now Reduced To Just Nine, CM Jitan Ram Manjhi on Tuesday Relocated The Portfolios Keeping 18 Important Departments with Him.
Manjhi will now handle Home, Finance, Cabinet Secretariat, General Administration, Vigilance, Election, Energy, SC and St Welfare, Information and Public Relations, Disaster Management, Commercial Management, Building and Build Ruction, Water Resources, Environment and Forests, Health, Sugarcane Industries, backward and extramely backward welfare as well as animal husbandry and fisheries departments.

He also expanded the work of other colleagues by allotting them two additional departments apart from that previous help by them.
