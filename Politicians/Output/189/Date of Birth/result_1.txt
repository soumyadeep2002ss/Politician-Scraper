Satish Kumar, (born July 1st, 1972) is an Indian politician and a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party. He’s won the 2014 Indian general elections from the Aligarh constituency of Uttar Pradesh.[1] [2] [3] [4]

Early life and education[edit]

Satish Kumar was born to Damodar Gautam and Ram Devi on 1 July 1972 in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. Satish Kumar has completed his education till the matriculation level.[5]

Political career[edit]
In May 2014 elected to 16th Lok Sabha[4]
In May 2019 elected to 17th Lok Sabha
Member of standing committee: Chemicals & fertilizers from September 2014 to July 2015 [6]
From September 2014 to 23 July 2015: Member, Standing Committee on Chemicals and Fertilizers[citation needed]
Member, Consultative Committee, Ministry of Railways[citation needed]
23 July 2015 onward: Member, Standing Committee on Labour[citation needed]
Personal life[edit]

Satish Kumar married Meenakshi Gautam on 30 April 1993. He is fond of sports, particularly Kabaddi.[7]
