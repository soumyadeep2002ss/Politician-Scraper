Malsawmtluanga (born 11 January 1989) simply known as Mala, is an Indian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Royal Wahingdoh F.C. in the I-League.He currently play with Aizawl FC.

Career[edit]

Mala started his career with Royal Wahingdoh and played for them in the Shillong Premier League and I-League 2nd Division.[1] In 2014, he helped the side reach the I-League.[2]

He made his professional debut for the side on 28 December 2014 in the Federation Cup against Mumbai.[3]
