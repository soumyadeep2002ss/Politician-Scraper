Celebration in Vindhya when Rajendra Shukla becomes deputy CM: Become a minister as many times as he wins; Know the political journey of deputy CM
Aaryan Puneet Dwivedi | Rewa princely 11 dec 2023 12:34 pm gmt
Mohan Yadav's name has been approved as the new Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh. At the same time, Vindhya's strongman leader and MLA Rajendra Shukla has been made deputy CM for 5th time from Rewa.

In the 2023 assembly elections, the BJP is forming the government once again by winning 163 seats. This time the Chief Minister's face in the government is in the tenure of MLA from Ujjain South and Shivraj SarkarDr. Mohan Yadav is the Minister of Higher Education. Along with Dr. Yadav, two deputy CMs have also been made in the state. Which includes four -time cabinet minister Rajendra Shukla and Jagdish Deora, Finance Minister in the Shivraj government. Leaders of MLAs Rajendra Shukla, Gaurishankar Bisen and Rahul Lodhi were made ministers in the Shivraj government.
