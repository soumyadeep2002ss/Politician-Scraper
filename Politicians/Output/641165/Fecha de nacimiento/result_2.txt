José García Pérez (26 January 1936 – 19 June 2022) was a Spanish politician.

A member of the Union of the Democratic Centre and the Andalusian Party, he served in the Congress of Deputies from 1977 to 1982.[1]

García died of liver cancer in Málaga on 19 June 2022.[2]
