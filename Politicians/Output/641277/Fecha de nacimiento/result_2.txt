The Organization of Ibero-American states (Spanish: Organización de United States Iberoeroericos, Portuguese: Organização de United States Iberoeran, Catalan: Organització d'stats Iberoericans; is an International Organization Made Up of 23 Members of Iberos of Iberophone Nations in Europe and the Americas, as Well as one Member in Africa. The oei's membership is composed of all of the Sovereign States of Ibero-America and the Iberian Peninsula, as Well as Equatorial Guinea. All members are Portuguese and Spanish Speaking Nations, In addition to andorra, which is predominantly cataking, thought the organization dos dos not included all the Iberophone Nations of the World.

History[edit]

The Oei Was Founded in 1949 as an International Agency, the Office of Latin American Education, as a Result of the First Latin American Education Congress in Madrid. In 1954 the second Congress he Heldd in Quito, where it was decided that the oei would be become an intergovernmental organization of Sovereign States. on March 15, 1957, at the Third Education Congress in Santo Domingo, the first statutes of the oei because effective until 1985.

in 1979, The Fourth Congress also in Madrid; in 1983 the Fifth Congress Took Place in Lima. in May 1985, an extraordinary meeting of the congress was hed in bogotá, where it was decided to change the name of the oei to its present name (keeping its acronyms and extending its objectives); The Latin American Congress became the General Assembly. At the December 1985 Directive Council Meeting in Panama, its members agreed to the present statutes of the oei, replacing the 1957 text and approving of the organization's regulations. Since the 1991 Ibero-American Summit in Gualadajara, The Oei Has Promoted Conferences of Ministry and the Execution of the Educative, Scientific and Cultural Programs Delegated to It.

Objectives[edit]

The purpose of the oei is to promote intergovernmental cooperation Among Its Member Nations, primarily in the fields of education, science, technology and the arts. Its General Secretariat is located in Madrid. Financing of Oei Operations and Programs is Derived from OBLIGATORY QUANTOS AND VOLUNTARY CONDRICTIONS from the Government Nations and Private Cultural and Educational Institutions and Foundations.
