English: Jaime Alberto Cabal Sanclemente is a Colombian politician and diplomat. He served as Minister of Economic Development (1999-2000) and as Colombia's Ambassador to Austria (2014-2018) and South Korea (2011-2014). In June 2018, he was appointed Deputy Secretary-General of the World Tourism Organization.
