Hon’ble Mr. Justice Prasanna B. Varale
 
Hon’ble Mr. Justice Prasanna B. Varale
Designation:
Hon'ble Chief Justice of Karnataka and Administrative Judge Of City Civil Court, Bengaluru

Hon’ble Mr. Justice Prasanna B. Varale: Born on 23rd June, 1962 at Nipani Graduated in Arts and Law from Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University. Enrolled as an Advocate on 12th August, 1985. Joined Chamber of Advocate Shri.S.N.Loya and practiced on Civil & Criminal Side. Served as lecturer in Law at Ambedkar Law College, Aurangabad from 1990 to 1992. Also worked as Assistant Government Pleader and Additional Public Prosecutor, High Court Bench at Aurangabad AND Additional Standing Counsel for Union Of India. Elevated at Bombay High Court on 18/07/2008 and His Lordship took oath as The Chief Justice of High Court of Karnataka on 15th October 2022.
