The 12th Cape Town Convention Academic Project Conference is taking place in Cambridge and online on 27-28 September. The Conference will focus on Enforcement and Dispute Resolution in relation to the #CapeTownConvention . It will be preceded on 26 September by a moot court involving students from Oxford and Cambridge Universities.

The Cape Town Convention Academic Project (CTCAP), which facilitates the study of the Cape Town Convention, operates under the joint auspices of #UNIDROIT and the Cambridge Faculty of Law, and the sponsorship of the Aviation Working Group. The Conference is kindly sponsored by Clifford Chance, Holland & Knight LLP, Sierra, RNC Legal and Blakes.

Learn more and register now at https://bit.ly/44JhWmM
