Nagar Singh Chouhan
A graduate from MP Bhoj Open University Bhopal he was born on August 2, 1978 in Alirajpur. He started his political journey from BJYM and went on to become the Alirajpur municipality chairman. He was fielded by the BJP from the Alirajpur ST assembly seat in 2003 which he won. He retained the seat in 2008 and won again in 2023.
Pradyuman Singh Tomar
Born on January 1, 1968 in village Navali in Ambah of Morena district, he is a graduate. Tomar is a four-time MLA who has won from Gwalior. He had served as power minister in Shivraj Singh Chouhan government. Tomar, is a supporter of Jyotiraditya Scindia, who had switched to BJP along with 22 MLAs in 2020.
Rakesh Shukla
He completed his graduation from Maharaja Mansingh College Gwalior Jiwaji University, Gwalior. He was born on April 1, 1966 in Mehgaon in Bhind district. He has been elected to the state assembly for the third term (2003, 2008 and 2023). Assets Rs 1.71 cr.
Chaitanya Kashyap
Born on January 16, 1959 in Indore, he won his seat from Ratlam city. A businessman by profession he is a third time MLA who won in 2013, 2018 and 2023. Kashyap was in the news for having donated his pay, perks and pension he received as an MLA for public welfare. He has assets worth Rs 296 crore and is among the richest ministers in the state.
Inder Singh Parmar
Born on August 1, 1964, Parmar served as school education minister in the Shivraj Singh Chouhan cabinet. He has been elected to the state assembly for the third term (2013, 2018 and 2023) from Shujalpur seat. He was associated with the ABVP during his college days in Ujjain. As minister of state for school education he had worked for adopting the new education policy in the schools. He has declared assets of over Rs 7 crore.
Ministers of state (Mos independent charge)
Krishna Gaur
Born on September 26, 1968, she is the daughter-in-law of former chief minister and senior BJP leader late Babulal Gaur who had represented Govindpura seat for 8 terms and Bhopal South for two terms. Krishna Gaur had also served as mayor of Bhopal in 2009 and was elected to the MP assembly from Govindpura seat for the first time in 2018. She was re-elected in 2023 by a huge margin of 1.06 lakh votes. She has declared assets worth over Rs 10 crore.
Dharmendra Singh Lodhi
He was born on September 5, 1978 in Nohta in Damoh district. Lodhi has been elected to the MP assembly for the second term (2018 and 2023) from Jabera seat in Damoh. He is a post graduate and is an agriculturist by profession. He has declared assets worth Rs 1.25 crore.
Dilip Jaiswal
He has been elected to the assembly from Kotma and is a first timer. He studied till BA first year in 1980 from Govt. Maharaj Martand Mahavidyalay Kotma, Avdhesha Pratap Singh University Rewa. He had defeated the former Congress MLA Sunil Saraf. An agriculturist by profession, Jaiswal has declared assets worth Rs 7.29 crore.
Gautam Tetwal
Born on September 5, 1963 in Sarangpur in Rajgarh district. A businessman, he is a science graduate and a second time (2008 and 2023) MLA from Sarangpur seat. He was initiated into RSS at the age of 14 and took to ABVP during his college days in Sehore. He has declared assets worth Rd 89 lakh.
Lakhan Patel
67 years old Patel is from the Pathariya constituency, with PG in agriculture. A 2nd time MLA, he has declared assets of Rs 4.7 crore and a criminal case against him. He had won the election in 2013 but lost in 2018. He also served with the State Bank of India but quit the job.
