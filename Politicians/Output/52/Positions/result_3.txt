Minister of Higher Education, Labour and Skills Development.
President	Mohamed Muizzu


Mariyam Mariya is a Maldivian politician who is Minister of Higher Education, Labour and Skills Development.[1] She was serving as Visiting Scholar at Maldives National University.[2]
