José María Román Guerrero (Chiclana de la Frontera, April 25, 1958) is a Spanish biologist and politic more voted list of the city in 2015, 2019 and 2023.

Roman is a biologist specializing in marine crops. He was director of the Environment Agency in Cádiz and Provincial Delegate of the Environment of the Junta de Andalucía.

He is also a secondary education professor at the IES Fernando Quiñones (Chiclana).

In 1995 he assumed the first possession of the mayor's office, and, in 1999 the spokesman of the Socialist Municipal Group in the City of Chiclana. He has been mayor of the city between June 2004 and June 2007, between November 2008 and May 2011, and from June 2015 to the present.

He also presided over the Commonwealth of Municipalities of the Bay of Cádiz from January 2016 to September 2019.

Finally, he was a provincial deputy of Finance between 2003 and 2007, and first vice president of the Diputación de Cádiz between 2019 and 2023, where he was in charge of the area of strategic, productive and social coordination and development.
