About

"Cooperation and sustainability in Andalusia: debates and practices. Local governments, public services, urban management, social intervention, development agendas and European framework of action", is a publication of works produced within the framework of the Specialization and Action Specialization Diploma Municipal International ”. It is a publication of the FAMSI and UNIA, with the co -financing of the Diputación de Jaén and the Andalusian Agency for International Development Cooperation, and the collaboration of Platforma and the European Commission. This is a selection of the final works of the second 'Diploma of Specialization in International Cooperation and Action' held in the 2020s and 2021, in order to favor training, critical reflection and the approach of alternatives on everyday practice in cooperation.
