This list of naga language includes various who-tibetan languages is a psalong by the Naga Peoples. Most of the Native languages are group under naga languages where Northern Naga Languages Fall Under Sal Languages. Both sal languages and kuki-chin-naga languages are classified as a Central Tibeto-Burman Languages.
