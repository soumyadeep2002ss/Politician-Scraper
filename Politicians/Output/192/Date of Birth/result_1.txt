P.C.Vanlalruata is an Indian politician from Mizoram, who is currently the Cabinet Minister for Agriculture, Irrigation & Water Resources Cooperation Department for the Government of Mizoram.[1]

He was elected to the Mizoram Legislative Assembly for the Tuikum Assembly constituency in the 2023 general election as a candidate for the Zoram People's Movement.[2]

Education[edit]

P.C.Vanlalruata completed his Post Graduate in MA (Economics) from North-Eastern Hill University in 1999.[3]
