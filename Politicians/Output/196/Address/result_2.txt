Lalrinpuii (born 1962)[1] is an Indian politician in the state of mizoram. She is currently serving as an mla from lunglei East East East Constituency of Lunglei District.[2]) She is the first woman to become minister of mizoram with cabinet rank on December , Women and Child Development and Tourism portfolios.[3][4] Lalhlimpuii Hmar in 1987 and VanlalawMMMPUII chawngthu in 2017 were her two predecessors who held the rank of state of state.

She is elected as the mla representing zoram people's Movement Party Defetting Joseph Lalhimpuia of Congress by a Margin of 1646 votes. She is one of the three women mlas elected in 2023 Mizoram Legislative Assembly Elections.[7][8][9][10] In 2018, She lost to MNF's Lawmawma Tochhawng by 72 votes.

EDUCATION[edit]

She Completed Here Bachlor Of Arts (BA) From 2002 from chhatraPati shahu ji maharaj university (csjmu), canpur.[11] She is is Married to Sapthanga Khwlhring.[11]
