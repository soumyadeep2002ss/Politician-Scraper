Employment

FAO is dedicated to recruiting the best staff possible, hiring internationally minded people to carry out its work toward the eradication of hunger. We invite you to explore the opportunities and benefits of working with us.
FAO does not charge a fee at any stage of its recruitment process (application, interview, processing, training, visa or other fee). For more information click here.

To view latest vacancies at FAO, visit the employment page.

Publications

All publications are available in the FAO Document Repository. They can be accessed through the Publications page.

For enquiries concerning reuse and distribution of FAO content, please contact copyright@fao.org . 

More information can be found in the About FAO publishing section.

Print copies of FAO publications can be purchased from the Organization's official distributors and global network of agents and booksellers.
