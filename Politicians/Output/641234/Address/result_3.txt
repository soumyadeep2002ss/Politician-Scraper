José Antonio Serrano Martínez (Tocinos Puente, 1959) is a Spanish doctor, professor and politic

Biography [edit]

Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery from the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Murcia, Doctor of Medicine and Surgery from the same university. He is an honorary collaborating professor at the Faculty of Medicine, a specialist in Family and Community Medicine and Medical of Sports Medicine by the Ministry of Culture and Education for the Planning of Sports Facilities.

It is a hospital tutor of resident doctors of family and community medicine at the Morales Meseguer hospital in the city of Murcia, and head of the Emergency Service in the same center.2

In the municipal elections of 2019 he was appointed head of the PSOE to the Mayor's Office of Murcia, obtaining his candidacy 28.91% of the votes and 9 councilors, three more than in the previous call. Thanks to a motion of censure against José Ballesta, supported by Citizens-Party of citizenship and Podemos, he took the Mayor's Office of Murcia on March 25, 2021.3 4
