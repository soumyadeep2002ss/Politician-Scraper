Composition of the municipal government

Elche City Council Corporation for Legislature 2023-2027

As a result of the elections held on May 28, 2023, a new municipal corporation was established.

The mayor-president of the Corporation is Mr. Pablo Ruz Villanueva, of the Popular Party, backed by the vote of 14 of the 27 councilors that form the Ilicitana Corporation, which was established on June 17, 2023.
