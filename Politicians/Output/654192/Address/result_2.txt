Peer-review
All medieval networks e-books are undergoing peer-review according to fashion
the "double blind". The names of the referee are inserted in the list, regularly
Daily, readable to the address: http://www.rmojs.unina.it/index.php/rm/about/dis-
PlayMeMenship/4.
The opinions of the references are archived.
All Published e-books are double-blind peer reviewed at least by Two Referees. Their
List is regularly updated at url: http://www.rmojs.unina.it/index.php/rm/about/dis-
PlayMeMenship/4.
Their Reviews Are Archived.
